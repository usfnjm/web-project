About Us

Pets.LB is the largest specialty pet retailer of services and solutions for the lifetime needs of pets. At Pets.LB, we love pets, and we believe pets make us better people. Every day with every connection, Pets.LB’s passionate associates help bring pet parents closer to their pets so they, together, can live more fulfilled lives. This vision impacts everything we do for our customers, the way we support our associates and how we give back to our communities.
